---
name: content-backlog
description: Turn completed findings and recommendations into an operator-ready remediation backlog.
---

When invoked:
1. Inspect the report findings, recommendations, and current content asset inventory first.
2. Group actions into create, revise, expand, and monitor.
3. Prioritize by business impact, dependency order, and evidence strength.
4. Avoid generic content advice; tie every backlog item to a specific visibility gap or comparison loss.
5. Output a concise backlog with rationale and suggested owner type.
