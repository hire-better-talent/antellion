# AI Visibility Diagnostic — Services Agreement (Short Form)

> **⚠️ NOT LEGAL ADVICE.** This template is designed for the $4,900 AI Visibility Diagnostic engagement and is intentionally compact for sub-$10K transactions that do not warrant full MSA execution. Have the first 2-3 actual executions reviewed by an attorney to confirm enforceability and alignment with the long-form MSA at `docs/msa-template.md`. Enterprise customers who require full MSA execution should be routed to the long-form MSA + SOW path instead of this short-form.

---

## How to use this template

- This file is the **source template**. Keep it versioned in the repo.
- For each new engagement: copy this file, rename it `sow-diagnostic-[customer]-[date].md`, fill in the bracketed placeholders, and strip all **Drafter's Note** callouts before sending.
- This is a complete, standalone services agreement. No separate MSA execution is required to enforce it.
- Bracketed fields are intentionally explicit. Search for `[` to find every field requiring input.
- Target final length after placeholder fill-in: 2 pages.

## 1. Services and Deliverables

### 1.1 Engagement

Antellion will deliver an **AI Visibility Diagnostic** for Customer covering one (1) job category ([CATEGORY NAME]), benchmarked against three (3) named talent competitors: [COMPETITOR 1], [COMPETITOR 2], and [COMPETITOR 3], scored across three (3) candidate personas curated by Antellion in consultation with Customer from the standard persona archetype library: [PERSONA 1], [PERSONA 2], [PERSONA 3].

### 1.2 Methodology

The Diagnostic comprises 40 candidate-intent queries spanning the four candidate journey stages (Discovery, Consideration, Evaluation, Commitment), each query rendered through the three named personas and run across four AI models (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity), at one (1) layer of conversational depth (single-turn). Total captured model responses: 480.

The full product specification — including persona archetypes, scoring methodology, and finding categories — is set forth in `docs/offers/ai-visibility-diagnostic.md` version dated [SPEC VERSION DATE], which is incorporated by reference.

### 1.3 Deliverables

Antellion will deliver:

1. A comprehensive **Diagnostic Report** (PDF and interactive HTML at a tokenized URL) containing at least ten (10) material findings, each with a named issue, captured data evidence, and an actionable category;
2. An executive **Findings Brief** suitable for circulation to the Customer's CEO, CMO, or board;
3. A **45-minute analyst readout call** scheduled within five (5) business days of report delivery; and
4. A **Finding Audit Appendix** within the Diagnostic Report enumerating each finding and its conformance to the materiality criteria in Section 1.4.

### 1.4 Materiality Criteria

A "**material finding**" is an observation in the final Diagnostic Report that satisfies all three of the following:

- **Specific named issue** — identifies a specific query, persona, model, competitor, or citation source;
- **Data evidence** — cites at least one captured model response or citation; and
- **Actionable category** — indicates a remediation direction.

### 1.5 Delivery Window

Antellion will deliver the Diagnostic Report within **ten (10) business days** of Customer's payment of the Fee under Section 2.

## 2. Fee and Payment

**Fee:** $4,900 USD, paid in full on signature, **Net 15** from invoice date.

The Fee is non-refundable except as expressly provided under the guarantee in Section 3 or for Antellion's failure to deliver in material breach of this Agreement. Invoices will be issued via Stripe. Accepted payment methods: ACH bank transfer (preferred), wire transfer, or credit card. Credit card payments are subject to a 3% surcharge for invoices over $5,000 (not applicable to this engagement).

---

## 3. Win Your Money Back Guarantee

If the final Diagnostic Report does not include at least **ten (10) material findings** as defined in Section 1.4, Customer is entitled to a full refund of the $4,900 Fee within ten (10) business days of Antellion's receipt of Customer's written notice. Antellion will document each finding's conformance to the materiality criteria in the Finding Audit Appendix; the count of material findings is determined by reference to that appendix.

If Customer identifies a factual error in the Diagnostic Report, Antellion will correct the error within forty-eight (48) hours of written notice or refund the Fee at Customer's election.

This guarantee is the sole and exclusive remedy for any failure of the Diagnostic to satisfy the deliverables stated in Section 1.

---

## 4. Rollover Credit

One hundred percent (100%) of the $4,900 Fee paid under this Agreement is creditable toward any of the following Antellion engagements executed within sixty (60) calendar days of Customer's acceptance (or deemed acceptance under Section 5) of the Diagnostic Report:

- Antellion Baseline Audit ($14,000)
- Antellion Baseline + Action Brief ($24,000)
- Any Antellion Phase 2 engagement (Focused, Strategic, or Enterprise)

The rollover credit applies once per Customer, is non-transferable, and is applied as a single line item on the deposit invoice for the subsequent engagement. The credit is forfeited if the subsequent engagement is terminated for Customer convenience after the credit has been applied.

---

## 5. Acceptance

Customer is deemed to have accepted the Diagnostic Report ten (10) business days after Antellion's delivery unless Customer provides written notice of rejection within that period citing a specific failure to meet the deliverables in Section 1.3 or the materiality criteria in Section 1.4.

---

## 6. Confidentiality

Each Party agrees to use the other Party's non-public information ("**Confidential Information**") solely to perform its obligations under this Agreement, to protect such information using at least reasonable care, and not to disclose it to any third party except to employees, contractors, and advisors with a need to know who are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as protective. The obligations in this Section 6 do not apply to information that is publicly known through no fault of the receiving Party, was already known to the receiving Party, is independently developed without use of the disclosing Party's Confidential Information, or is required to be disclosed by law (provided prompt notice is given to permit a protective response). The confidentiality obligations under this Section 6 survive for **three (3) years** after the conclusion of the engagement.

---

Antellion retains all rights, title, and interest in its underlying methodology, query libraries, scoring frameworks, software, and the persona archetype library. Customer may not resell, redistribute publicly, or repurpose the deliverables outside Customer's organization without Antellion's prior written consent.

EXCEPT FOR BREACHES OF SECTION 6 (CONFIDENTIALITY) OR SECTION 7 (INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY), EACH PARTY'S TOTAL CUMULATIVE LIABILITY ARISING OUT OF OR RELATED TO THIS AGREEMENT WILL NOT EXCEED THE FEE PAID BY CUSTOMER UNDER SECTION 2. IN NO EVENT WILL EITHER PARTY BE LIABLE FOR ANY INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, SPECIAL, EXEMPLARY, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES, OR FOR LOST PROFITS, LOST REVENUE, OR LOST BUSINESS OPPORTUNITY, REGARDLESS OF THE FORM OF ACTION.

## 9. Disclaimers

Antellion does not warrant any specific business, hiring, employer-brand, or AI-visibility outcome. Antellion's deliverable is the Diagnostic Report itself, scoped per Section 1, and the guarantee in Section 3 is the exclusive measure of Antellion's deliverable obligation. AI model outputs are inherently variable; the Diagnostic captures a snapshot of model behavior at the time of scan and does not guarantee that subsequent queries by Customer or third parties will return identical results.
