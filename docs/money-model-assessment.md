# Money Model Assessment — Antellion vs Hormozi Framework

**Date:** 2026-04-23
**Context:** 6 days to launch. Assessing current offer architecture against $100M Money Models.

---

## 1. Attraction Offer

**Current:** Free Snapshot. Strong as a lead magnet, weak as a money model — it generates zero cash, so it cannot fund the acquisition of "two more customers."

**What to change:** Keep the Snapshot (it's our cold email hook), but add a **paid attraction offer** above Baseline that's the real front door: the **"AI Visibility Diagnostic" — $4,900, 10 business days.** This is Baseline's refund guarantee reframed as *Win Your Money Back*: "If we don't surface at least 10 material findings, full refund. If you proceed to Baseline, the $4,900 is credited." It gets card-on-file at enterprise-safe pricing (signable on a P-card or simple PO), proves the motion, and sets up a mandatory rollover into Baseline. This is the single highest-leverage change.

**Copy (website / sales):** "Start with a $4,900 AI Visibility Diagnostic. If we surface fewer than 10 findings, you pay nothing. If the findings warrant deeper work, your $4,900 rolls into the Baseline Audit."

**Do NOT:** Run giveaways, free-trial-with-penalty, or public discounted pricing. Do not turn the Snapshot into a paid offer — it's working as top-of-funnel.

---

## 2. Upsell Offers

**Current:** Baseline → Phase 2 exists conceptually. Three-tier Phase 2 menu (Focused / Strategic / Enterprise) is a proper **Menu Upsell**, but the **Rollover is not formalized** and there is no **Anchor**.

**What to change — two moves:**

**(a) Formalize the Rollover.** Contractually, "100% of the Baseline fee credits toward any Phase 2 engagement booked within 60 days of findings delivery." Write it into the Baseline SOW. This turns the $14K Baseline from a standalone deliverable into a down-payment on Phase 2, which is exactly the Hormozi mechanic. Rolling $14K toward a $89K+ engagement satisfies the "next offer must be 4x the credit" rule.

**(b) Add an Anchor above Enterprise.** Introduce **"Antellion Board-Level Engagement — $275K"**: quarterly CHRO/CEO visibility briefings, competitor war-gaming, named analyst, annual board deck. Present it FIRST in Phase 2 conversations. It makes $124K Enterprise feel like the reasonable choice, and it's a real offer you'd actually deliver if someone said yes. Magnetic-middle the existing three tiers by pricing Strategic closer to Enterprise (e.g., $104K → $109K) so buyers round up.

**BAMFAM:** Every Snapshot delivery call books the Diagnostic kickoff. Every Diagnostic findings call books the Baseline. Every Baseline findings call books Phase 2 scoping. No engagement ends without the next meeting on calendar.

**Do NOT:** Do one-click upsells, surprise charges, or pushy A/B choice-forcing. Enterprise buyers want choice architecture, not carnival pressure.

---

## 3. Downsell Offer

**Current:** None. If a prospect balks at $89K Focused, we have nowhere to go but "call us later."

**What to change:** Add a **Feature Downsell**, not a payment plan. Name it **"Baseline + Action Brief — $24,000"**: Baseline deliverable plus a 15-page prioritized remediation brief for one journey stage. No quarterly re-scans, no content assets, no competitor tracking. This catches buyers who want more than Baseline but can't approve $75K+ this quarter, and it preserves the rollover path — the $24K still credits toward a future Phase 2 engagement.

**Do NOT:** Seesaw payment plans, 0%-interest financing theater, or "free trial if you provide three references." All wrong for this buyer. Procurement will read these as desperation signals and it damages the premium positioning.

---

## 4. Continuity Offer

**Current:** Deferred. Quarterly re-scans are bundled as finite deliverables inside Phase 2 tiers. This is the biggest gap against the framework — no recurring revenue means CAC has to be recovered entirely from project work.

**What to change — this week, on paper only (don't launch until May):** Design **"Antellion Continuous — $6,500/month, 12-month term"** as a post-Phase-2 continuity offer. Quarterly re-scans, monthly monitoring alerts, one executive briefing per quarter, Slack access for findings questions. Use the **Waived Fee** mechanic: "$25K activation fee, waived with 12-month commitment." Use the **Continuity Discount**: "Commit at Phase 2 signing and the first quarterly re-scan is included free."

The pricing math: if standalone quarterly re-scans were offered at $13K (roughly 2x the monthly-equivalent), the 2x ratio gets you ~70% attach per Hormozi's table. Structure the Phase 2 SOW so Continuous is presented on the same signature page.

**Do NOT:** Launch Continuous before we've delivered 2-3 Baselines and know what "monitoring" actually means operationally. Announcing a subscription we can't service is worse than not having one.

## Top 5 Prioritized Actions — This Week

1. **Formalize the Baseline → Phase 2 rollover in the SOW template.** One paragraph, 30 minutes of legal/contract work. Highest ROI change in the entire stack. *(docs/sow-template.md)*
2. **Write the $4,900 AI Visibility Diagnostic offer page + SOW.** This is the paid attraction offer and the real money-model engine. Launch-week deliverable. Keeps pricing off the public site — this lives in sales conversations and post-Snapshot emails.
3. **Create the $275K Board-Level Engagement anchor on a one-pager.** Don't publish. Use in Phase 2 scoping calls to reset the price frame. Half a day of work.
4. **Add the $24K Baseline + Action Brief feature downsell to the pricing menu.** One SOW variant. Catches deals that would otherwise die between Baseline and Focused.
5. **Draft Antellion Continuous on paper (pricing, inclusions, waived-fee mechanic) but do NOT launch.** Get it signable by June 1. This is the long-term compounding play — every Phase 2 close should have the Continuous option on the same page by Q3.

**What I'd explicitly defer:** Weekly billing tricks, aggressive BAMFAM scripting, payment plan cascades, ACH-vs-card optimization. These matter at volume; we need 3-5 paying customers first.
