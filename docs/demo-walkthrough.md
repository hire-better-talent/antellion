# Demo Walkthrough: Meridian Technologies

## The Demo Client

**Meridian Technologies** is a mid-market enterprise software company (~2,000 employees) in Austin, TX. They build supply chain optimization and logistics intelligence products for Fortune 500 manufacturers. Recently raised Series D, scaling AI/ML capabilities. Engineering org is ~400 people.

This is the kind of company that would buy an AI Employer Visibility Assessment: big enough to have a TA team and employer brand budget, actively hiring in competitive roles, and losing candidates to better-known competitors without understanding why.

## The Competitors

| Company | Size | Story in the Demo |
|---------|------|-------------------|
| **Apex Cloud Systems** | ~800 eng | The dominant competitor. Strong employer brand, high Glassdoor ratings, remote-first. 75% AI mention rate. The "wake-up call." |
| **NovaBridge Analytics** | ~500 | Technical reputation leader. Open-source, conference presence. 58% mention rate. Ahead on tech brand. |
| **VeloChain** | ~350 | Peer benchmark. Similar stage, similar mention rate (~42%). Shows Meridian isn't alone but isn't leading either. |
| **Forge Industrial** | ~600 | The laggard. 28% mention rate. Proves that weak AI presence is real and measurable. |

## The Visibility Gap Story

Meridian has a **47% AI mention rate** — moderate, not terrible, but candidates asking AI about employers in their space are more likely to hear about Apex Cloud first.

### Theme-Level Breakdown (the "aha moment")

| Theme | Meridian | Story |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Hiring Process | 83% | **Strength.** Candidates who ask about the interview process hear about Meridian. |
| Role Expectations | 67% | **Solid.** Engineers researching what the work looks like find Meridian. |
| Engineering Culture | 50% | **Moderate.** Present but not dominant — Apex and NovaBridge win this. |
| Competitor Comparison | 50% | **Split.** Meridian shows up in head-to-head queries but not "best of" lists. |
| Compensation | 17% | **Critical gap.** Candidates asking about pay almost never hear about Meridian. Apex and NovaBridge dominate. |
| Culture & WLB | 17% | **Critical gap.** Candidates asking about culture, DEI, and work-life balance don't find Meridian. Apex wins this decisively. |

### Citation Gap Story

7 sources inform AI about competitors but not Meridian:
- levels.fyi, builtin.com, comparably.com, teamblind.com, wellfound.com, payscale.com, techcrunch.com

These are the platforms where compensation data, employer reviews, and company profiles shape what AI tells candidates. Meridian has no presence on most of them.

**Say:** "Look at this one: 'best paying enterprise software companies for engineers.' Apex is mentioned. NovaBridge is mentioned. Meridian is not. The AI cites Levels.fyi and Blind — platforms where Meridian has no presence."
