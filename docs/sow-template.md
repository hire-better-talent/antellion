# Statement of Work — Template

> **⚠️ NOT LEGAL ADVICE.** This template is designed to be executed under a signed Master Services Agreement (MSA). Have the first 2-3 actual SOWs reviewed by an attorney to confirm alignment with the Antellion MSA and any customer-specific procurement requirements. Do not execute this SOW standalone without an MSA in place.

---

## How to use this template

- This file is the **source template**. Keep it versioned in the repo.
- For each new engagement: copy this file, rename it `sow-[customer]-[tier]-[date].md`, fill in the bracketed placeholders, delete inapplicable scope blocks, and strip all **Drafter's Note** callouts before sending.
- Pick the scope block that matches the engagement tier from the **Scope Library** at the bottom of this file. Paste into Section 3. Delete the Library before sending.
- Each SOW is signed separately from the MSA. Under the **two-phase architecture default (Model A)**, Phase 1 Baseline and Phase 2 are separate SOWs signed sequentially.
- Bracketed fields are intentionally explicit. Search for `[` to find every field requiring input.

## Scope Block A — Baseline Audit

### 3.1 Services

Antellion will deliver a **Baseline Audit** covering **one (1) job category** ([CATEGORY NAME]) across the full candidate decision journey (Discovery, Consideration, Evaluation, and Commitment stages), tested across **three (3) AI models**: ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini. The Audit includes competitive analysis against [NUMBER — typically 3] named competitors: [COMPETITOR LIST].

The Audit includes:

- Narrative consistency scoring across queries and models
- Human interpretation of findings with remediation prioritization

### 4.1 Deliverables

### 4.2 Completeness Criteria

The final Report must include at least:

- **Ten (10) specific findings** spanning all four (4) candidate journey stages

## Scope Block B — Focused Audit (Phase 2)

### 3.1 Services

Antellion will deliver a **Focused Audit** covering **one (1) job category** ([CATEGORY NAME]) with **multi-turn conversational probing at three (3) layers of depth** across the full candidate decision journey, tested across **three (3) AI models**: ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini. The Audit includes detailed competitive analysis against [NUMBER — typically 3] named competitors: [COMPETITOR LIST].

This SOW presumes the completion of a prior Phase 1 Baseline Audit delivered under SOW No. [PHASE 1 SOW NUMBER] on [PHASE 1 DELIVERY DATE]. Findings from Phase 1 inform Phase 2 scope and remediation priorities.

### 3.2 Methodology

Phase 2 Focused Audit methodology includes:

- Multi-turn conversational probing (3 layers of conversational depth per query thread) across all 4 journey stages and all 3 AI models
- Cross-layer consistency analysis identifying where AI narratives shift under follow-up questioning
- Hallucination and source-drift mapping showing how wrong narratives compound across a candidate research session
- Full citation ecosystem audit mapping every platform AI cites for Customer and each Named Competitor
- Industry narrative mapping positioning Customer against market peers on talent-specific dimensions

### 4.1 Deliverables

1. **Written Report** — 40+ page PDF, shareable within Customer's organization
2. **Board-Ready Slide Deck** — 10 slides, editable source files included
3. **90-Day Prioritized Remediation Roadmap** — specific platforms, content types, owners, and sequencing
4. **Two 60-minute Executive Readout Calls** — initial findings session and 30-day implementation check-in
5. **Two Q&A sessions** (30 minutes each) within 90 days of deliverable acceptance
6. **Quarterly Re-scans (4 total) for 12 months** — Snapshot-depth refreshes to measure narrative drift

### 4.2 Completeness Criteria

The final Report must include at least:

- **Fifteen (15) specific findings** spanning all four (4) candidate journey stages
- **Eight (8) prioritized remediation actions** with named platform, content type, and channel for each
- A documented cross-layer divergence analysis showing at least **three (3) instances** where AI's narrative shifted under follow-up probing

- **Total Fee:** $75,000 (Phase 2 only; add $14,000 if combined with Phase 1 in a single SOW under MSA Model B)
- **Deposit:** $37,500 (Net 15 from SOW execution)
