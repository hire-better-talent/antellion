# Full Assessment Offer Stack

**Date created:** 2026-04-19
**Purpose:** Internal reference for Antellion's Snapshot + Full Assessment tier structure, value stacks, and risk reversals. Paste from this doc when building pitch decks, proposals, response emails, and readout materials.

---

## Core principles

1. **Risk reversal is tied exclusively to Antellion-controlled outcomes.** Refund triggers are defined by what *Antellion ships* — findings count, remediation actions surfaced, factual accuracy, delivery time, deliverable completeness. Never tied to what the customer does after delivery (implementation, AI visibility improvement, hiring outcomes, etc.).

2. **Value framing is honest, not inflated.** Component stand-alone values are grounded in realistic consulting rates. Sophisticated CPOs spot 3-5x inflated value stacks and discount the whole pitch. Aim for 1.4-1.6x defensible multiplier.

3. **Pricing is not published on the landing page.** Landing page exists to capture Snapshot leads. Pricing is introduced in follow-up conversations, pitch decks, and proposals — after value has been delivered or demonstrated.

4. **Every paid tier covers all 4 candidate journey stages by default.** Discovery, Consideration, Evaluation, Commitment. The journey is methodology, not a pricing lever. Pricing scales on categories × models × layers.

## Tier 1: The Visibility Snapshot (Free)

**Role in funnel:** Top-of-funnel lead magnet. The deliverable that turns a cold outreach into a warmed-up prospect.

**Scope:** 1 job category × 1 layer × 3 models × 100 queries (Discovery-heavy, with light sampling of other stages).

### What's included

- 100 candidate-intent queries tested across ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini
- Mention rate vs three closest talent competitors
- Citation gap map — specific platforms AI cites for competitors that it does not cite for you
- Narrative consistency score — how stable AI's story about you is across queries and models
- Top 5 Discovery queries where competitors appeared and you did not (the queries losing candidates before they reach your careers page)
- Shareable executive summary via tokenized URL — no login required

### Bonus

- Optional 15-minute walkthrough call with Jordan to interpret the findings (no sales script, no call required)

### Delivery

- 48 hours from submission

### Notes

- No cost. No sales call required. Customer data is used only to produce the Snapshot.
- This is the only tier where a sales call is not built into the deliverable.

## Tier 2: Starter Audit — $14,000

**Role in funnel:** The paid entry point. Functions as the Snapshot → Full Assessment bridge AND as a downsell from the Focused Audit for budget-constrained buyers.

**Scope:** 1 job category × 1 layer × 3 models × all 4 candidate journey stages. Human-interpreted.

### What you get

1. **Full-journey AI analysis** — 300-400 queries across Discovery, Consideration, Evaluation, and Commitment stages, tested across ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini
2. **Competitive citation map** — the specific platforms AI is citing for each of your three closest competitors, and the gaps for you
3. **Source attribution analysis** — every source AI is drawing from, ranked by influence on the narrative about your company
4. **Prioritized remediation roadmap** — at least 5 specific platform-level actions ranked by effort × impact
5. **Executive-ready written report** (PDF, shareable with your team)
6. **60-minute executive readout call** — live walkthrough with Jordan
7. **One follow-up Q&A session** (30 min) within 30 days of delivery

### Delivery

- 2 weeks from scope confirmation

### Guarantee (Antellion-controlled)

If the final report does not include:
- At least **10 specific findings** across the 4 journey stages, and
- At least **5 prioritized remediation actions**

...full refund.

If a factual error is identified in the analysis, Antellion corrects it within 48 hours or refunds.

### Stand-alone component value

| Component | Stand-alone value |
|---|---|
| Core analysis (1 category, full journey, 3 models) | $10,000 |
| Written report + source attribution | $8,000 |
| Executive readout call | $2,000 |
| Follow-up Q&A | $1,000 |
| **Total** | **~$21,000** |

**You pay $14,000.**

## Tier 3: Focused Audit — $75,000

**Role in funnel:** The methodology flagship. The tier that demonstrates what Antellion does that no competitor can replicate.

**Scope:** 1 job category × 3 layers × 3 models × all 4 candidate journey stages.

### What you get

1. **Everything in the Starter Audit**, plus:
2. **Multi-turn conversational probing** — 3 layers of conversational depth per query thread. The only methodology that shows where AI's story about your company shifts when candidates probe further.
3. **Cross-layer consistency analysis** — where AI contradicts itself under follow-up questioning, where it drifts to new sources, where it entrenches competitive narratives
4. **Hallucination and source-drift map** — what AI gets wrong about you, and how wrong narratives compound over a candidate's research session
5. **Full citation ecosystem audit** — every platform AI draws from for your 3 closest competitors, with specific remediation paths for each gap
6. **Industry narrative mapping** — how AI positions your company against market peers on talent-specific dimensions
7. **90-day prioritized remediation roadmap** — specific platforms, specific content types, specific owners, specific sequencing
8. **Executive-ready written report** (40+ pages, PDF + shareable)
9. **Board-ready 10-slide deck** (editable source files included)
10. **Two 60-minute executive readout calls** — initial findings + 30-day check-in
11. **Two Q&A sessions** (30 min each) within 90 days
12. **Quarterly re-scan included for 12 months** — 4 Snapshot-depth refreshes to measure narrative drift

### Delivery

- 3 weeks from scope confirmation

### Guarantee (Antellion-controlled)

If the final report does not include all of the following, full refund:
- At least **15 specific findings** spanning all 4 candidate journey stages
- At least **8 prioritized remediation actions** with named platform, content type, and channel
- A documented cross-layer divergence analysis showing at least **3 instances** where AI's narrative shifted under follow-up probing

If a factual error is identified in the analysis, Antellion corrects it within 48 hours or refunds.

### Stand-alone component value

| Component | Stand-alone value |
|---|---|
| Multi-layer analysis (3 layers × 3 models × full journey) | $40,000 |
| Enhanced written report (40+ pages) | $15,000 |
| Board-ready deck | $5,000 |
| Two executive readout calls | $5,000 |
| Two Q&A sessions | $2,000 |
| Four quarterly re-scans (at discounted re-scan rate) | $40,000 |
| **Total** | **~$107,000** |

**You pay $75,000.**

## Tier 4: Strategic Audit — $90,000

**Role in funnel:** CPO-level multi-function engagement. Covers the 2-3 roles the buyer is most anxious about.

**Scope:** 3 job categories × 3 layers × 3 models × all 4 candidate journey stages.

### What you get

1. **Everything in the Focused Audit**, applied to three categories
2. **Cross-category pattern analysis** — how AI's narrative about your company shifts by role (e.g., how your engineering narrative differs from your sales narrative)
3. **Function-specific competitive sets** per category (different competitors per category if warranted — e.g., Stripe for engineering, Salesforce for sales)
4. **Per-category 90-day remediation roadmaps** — three parallel action plans with cross-category dependencies mapped
5. **Three 60-minute readout calls** — one per category, plus a rollup synthesis session
6. **Three follow-up Q&A sessions** total
7. **Quarterly re-scan included for 12 months** across all three categories

### Delivery

- 4 weeks from scope confirmation

### Guarantee (Antellion-controlled)

Focused-level guarantee applied **per category**:
- At least 15 findings and 8 remediation actions per function
- Cross-layer divergence analysis per category

If any category fails the guarantee, full refund on that category's portion of the engagement.

If a factual error is identified in the analysis, Antellion corrects it within 48 hours or refunds the affected portion.

### Stand-alone component value

| Component | Stand-alone value |
|---|---|
| Three Focused-equivalent analyses | $120,000 |
| Cross-category pattern analysis + rollup synthesis | $15,000 |
| Three executive readouts + rollup call | $8,000 |
| Three Q&A sessions | $3,000 |
| Quarterly re-scans (3 cats × 4 quarters at discounted rate) | $20,000 |
| **Total** | **~$166,000** |

**You pay $90,000.**

## Tier 5: Enterprise Audit — Starting at $110,000 (Custom)

**Role in funnel:** Fortune 500 / 10K+ headcount. Strategic engagement with ongoing advisory.

**Scope:** 5 or more job categories × 3 layers × 3 models × all 4 candidate journey stages, plus ongoing advisory.

### What you get

1. **Everything in the Strategic Audit**, across 5+ categories
2. **12 months of advisor-level access to Jordan** — monthly 60-minute strategy sessions for implementation guidance
3. **Custom research questions beyond standard methodology** — unique competitive dimensions, industry-specific AI visibility questions, M&A-related analysis, diversity narrative deep-dives
4. **Annual "State of AI Visibility" benchmark for your industry** (available when 5+ comparable competitors can be mapped)
5. **SOC2 / security review cooperation** for enterprise procurement
6. **Two live executive briefing presentations** — board-level or leadership team readouts (Jordan presents)

### Delivery

- 6 weeks from scope confirmation

### Guarantee (Antellion-controlled)

- Strategic-level guarantee applied per category
- Delivery-window guarantee: if the 6-week delivery slips more than 5 business days, 10% credit applied to any future engagement

### Pricing

- Custom quote based on category count, advisory scope, and custom research
- **Starts at $110,000**

## Usage guidance

### Where to use the value-stack framing

| Context | Show stand-alone value? |
|---|---|
| LinkedIn DM / cold email reply | **No** — reads pitchy in conversational format |
| Response template #9 (price question) | **Summary only** — tier names, prices, one-line description |
| Post-Snapshot delivery follow-up email | **Partial** — introduce tier structure without full value stack |
| Scheduled sales call | **Yes** — use pitch deck with full value stacks per tier |
| Written proposal | **Yes** — full value stack per recommended tier |
| Executive readout materials | **Yes** — anchored to specific recommendations |

### Referral rev share math (20% to referrer)

- Starter Audit ($14K) → $2,800 per referral
- Focused Audit ($75K) → $15,000 per referral
- Strategic Audit ($90K) → $18,000 per referral
- Enterprise Audit ($110K+) → $22,000+ per referral

### Delivery capacity (solo, before hiring a delivery analyst)

| Tier | Monthly capacity |
|---|---|
| Snapshot | 10-15 |
| Starter Audit | 4-5 |
| Focused Audit | 2 |
| Strategic Audit | 1-1.5 |
| Enterprise Audit | ~1 every 6 weeks |

**Realistic mixed load:** 1 Strategic OR 2 Focused per month, plus 2 Starter, plus 8-10 Snapshots. ~$160-180K monthly revenue ceiling pre-hire.

## Buyer psychology breakpoints (at 5K-employee CPO level)

| Price band | Dynamics |
|---|---|
| Under $15K | Discretionary spend, CPO signs same week. Dangerous zone — signals "vendor tool." |
| $15K-$50K | CPO approves, loops in finance. Sweet spot for pilot/land engagement. |
| $75K-$100K | Triggers procurement (4-6 week cycle). Requires board-level narrative. Reads as "strategic initiative." |
| $100K+ | Enterprise procurement, SOC2 questions, committee buying. Budget-cycle dependent. |

**The jump from $45K to $75K is the painful one.** Worth it — $75K reads "strategic initiative," $45K reads "line item." CPOs protect budgets that sound strategic; they cut line items.

## Notes for future revision

- Re-evaluate after 10-20 real customer engagements. Actual delivery hours per tier and customer-reported value perception will sharpen pricing.
- If Snapshot conversion to Starter Audit is <5%, consider price adjustment or additional bonus stacking on Starter.
- If Starter → Focused upgrade rate is <30%, investigate whether Focused value stack communicates the depth differentiation clearly enough.
- Published-pricing decision revisits at Month 4+ after observing whether "what does this cost?" dominates Snapshot follow-up objections.
