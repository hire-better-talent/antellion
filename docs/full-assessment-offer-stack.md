# Full Assessment Offer Stack

**Date created:** 2026-04-19
**Purpose:** Internal reference for Antellion's Snapshot + Full Assessment tier structure, value stacks, and risk reversals. Paste from this doc when building pitch decks, proposals, response emails, and readout materials.

---

## Core principles

1. **Every paid engagement begins with Phase 1: Baseline Audit.** Baseline is mandatory. Phase 2 (Focused / Strategic / Enterprise) is scoped and priced against Phase 1 findings. This is the McKinsey / BCG two-phase architecture: establish facts before committing to larger execution. The customer may also elect to stop at Baseline — it stands alone as a complete deliverable.

2. **Risk reversal is tied exclusively to Antellion-controlled outcomes.** Refund triggers are defined by what *Antellion ships* — findings count, remediation actions surfaced, factual accuracy, delivery time, deliverable completeness. Never tied to what the customer does after delivery (implementation, AI visibility improvement, hiring outcomes, etc.).

3. **Value framing is honest, not inflated.** Component stand-alone values are grounded in realistic consulting rates. Sophisticated CPOs spot 3-5x inflated value stacks and discount the whole pitch. Aim for 1.4-1.6x defensible multiplier.

4. **Pricing is not published on the landing page.** Landing page exists to capture Snapshot leads. Pricing is introduced in follow-up conversations, pitch decks, and proposals — after value has been delivered or demonstrated.

5. **Every paid tier covers all 4 candidate journey stages by default.** Discovery, Consideration, Evaluation, Commitment. The journey is methodology, not a pricing lever. Pricing scales on categories × models × layers.

## Tier 1: The Visibility Snapshot (Free)

**Role in funnel:** Top-of-funnel lead magnet. The deliverable that turns a cold outreach into a warmed-up prospect.

**Scope:** 1 job category × 1 layer × 3 models × 100 queries (Discovery-heavy, with light sampling of other stages).

### What's included

- 100 candidate-intent queries tested across ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini
- Mention rate vs three closest talent competitors
- Citation gap map — specific platforms AI cites for competitors that it does not cite for you
- Narrative consistency score — how stable AI's story about you is across queries and models
- Top 5 Discovery queries where competitors appeared and you did not (the queries losing candidates before they reach your careers page)
- Shareable executive summary via tokenized URL — no login required

### Bonus

- Optional 15-minute walkthrough call with Jordan to interpret the findings (no sales script, no call required)

### Delivery

- 48 hours from submission

### Notes

- No cost. No sales call required. Customer data is used only to produce the Snapshot.
- This is the only tier where a sales call is not built into the deliverable.

## Tier 1.5: AI Visibility Diagnostic — $4,900

**Role in funnel:** Paid attraction offer. The first paid step for buyers who want a concrete audit but are not ready to commit to the $14K Baseline budget cycle. Sized to land on a P-card or simple PO (sub-$10K, no committee approval typically required). Always rolls into Baseline via 100% credit.

**Scope:** 40 queries × 4 models × 3 personas × 1 layer × all 4 journey stages = 480 captured AI responses. 3 named competitors.

### What you get

- **18-25 page Diagnostic Report** (PDF + interactive HTML) with 10+ specific findings, each with captured data evidence and citation inventory
- **2-page Findings Brief** — scannable executive summary for CEO / CMO / board circulation
- **45-minute recorded analyst readout call** within 5 business days of delivery
- **Interactive HTML view** at a tokenized URL, shareable with the customer's team

### Delivery

- **10 business days** from deposit receipt (faster than Baseline's 2 weeks by design)

### Guarantee — Win Your Money Back (Antellion-controlled)

Full refund of the $4,900 fee if the Diagnostic Report does not include at least **10 material findings**. "Material finding" is defined in the deliverable (specific named issue + data evidence + actionable category) and presented in a Finding Audit Appendix so the buyer does not have to adjudicate the count.

Factual errors corrected within 48 hours or refunded.

### Rollover credit

**100% of the $4,900 credits toward any of the following signed within 60 days of Diagnostic delivery:**
- Phase 1 Baseline Audit ($14K → $9,100 net)
- Baseline + Action Brief ($24K → $19,100 net)
- Any Phase 2 engagement (Focused, Strategic, Enterprise)

Credit expires if the buyer does not proceed within 60 days. Buyer retains Diagnostic deliverables.

### What's explicitly NOT included (margin protection for Baseline)

- No Journey Action Map
- No prioritized remediation roadmap
- No multi-layer conversational probing
- No hallucination or source-drift analysis
- No quarterly re-scans or monitoring
- No content asset production

### Unit economics

~$2,950 delivery cost (18 analyst hrs + LLM + infra + processing) → ~$1,950 contribution margin (~40%) as a standalone offer. With 40% rollover to Baseline, effective revenue per Diagnostic ~$10,500.

## Phase 1: Baseline Audit — $14,000

**Role:** **Mandatory Phase 1 for every paid engagement.** Establishes the fact pattern Phase 2 is scoped against. Also stands alone as a complete deliverable for buyers who want only the Baseline without committing to Phase 2. The Baseline introduces the **Journey Action Map** — a strategic layer bridging raw AI visibility data to stage-specific remediation priorities. The Journey Action Map is a paid-tier deliverable and is not included in the free Snapshot.

**Scope:** 1 job category × 1 layer × 3 models × 3 category-specific personas × all 4 candidate journey stages. Human-interpreted.

### What you get

1. **Full-journey AI analysis** — 300-400 queries across Discovery, Consideration, Evaluation, and Commitment stages, tested across ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini, rendered through 3 candidate personas scoped to your named job category
2. **Competitive citation map** — the specific platforms AI is citing for each of your three closest competitors, and the gaps for you
3. **Source attribution analysis** — every source AI is drawing from, ranked by influence on the narrative about your company
4. **Journey Action Map** — a 1-2 page strategic scorecard mapping your AI visibility position across all four candidate journey stages, with the #1 remediation priority identified at each stage, tied to named platforms or citation sources
5. **Prioritized remediation roadmap** — at least 5 specific platform-level actions ranked by effort × impact (inclusive of the 4 stage-specific priorities from the Journey Action Map)
6. **Executive-ready written report** (PDF, shareable with your team)
7. **60-minute executive readout call** — live walkthrough with Jordan
8. **One follow-up Q&A session** (30 min) within 30 days of delivery

### Delivery

- 2 weeks from scope confirmation

### Guarantee (Antellion-controlled)

If the final report does not include:
- At least **10 specific findings** across the 4 journey stages;
- A **Journey Action Map** covering all 4 candidate journey stages, each with a clearly identified #1 remediation priority tied to a named platform or citation source; and
- At least **5 prioritized remediation actions** total (inclusive of the 4 stage-specific priorities from the Journey Action Map plus additional overall-priority actions)

...full refund.

**Additionally: the customer is never obligated to proceed to Phase 2.** Baseline can be the entire engagement if the findings do not warrant further work.

If a factual error is identified in the analysis, Antellion corrects it within 48 hours or refunds.

| Journey Action Map strategic synthesis | $6,000 |
| Written report + source attribution | $8,000 |
| Executive readout call | $2,000 |
| Follow-up Q&A | $1,000 |
| **Total** | **~$27,000** |

**You pay $14,000.**

### Rollover credit (forward to Phase 2)

**100% of the $14,000 Baseline fee credits toward any Phase 2 engagement or Baseline + Action Brief signed within 60 days** of Baseline deliverable acceptance. Applied as a credit memo on the Phase 2 deposit invoice. Non-transferable, one per customer, forfeited if Phase 2 is terminated for convenience after the credit is applied.

## Phase 1 Variant: Baseline + Action Brief — $24,000

**Role in funnel:** Feature downsell between Baseline and Focused. Offered when a customer pitches toward Phase 2 but encounters current-cycle budget resistance ("we could do $30K this quarter but not $90K"). Preserves the rollover path so adding the Action Brief does not cost the customer future Phase 2 budget.

**Scope:** Everything in Baseline, plus a **15-page prioritized remediation brief for one customer-selected candidate journey stage** and an additional 45-minute Action Brief readout call.

### What's added beyond Baseline

- Deep dive into one stage's citation ecosystem (vs Baseline's full-journey overview)
- Named platforms ranked by remediation priority (5-8 platforms) with content gap inventory
- Per-platform remediation direction with named platform owners
- 90-day execution sequence — week-by-week prioritization
- Additional 45-minute Action Brief readout call
- 15-page brief structure: context, platform rankings, gap inventory, remediation, sequence, measurement plan

### Delivery

- **3 weeks total** (2 weeks Baseline + 1 week Action Brief after customer's stage selection)

### Rollover credit

**100% of the $24,000 credits toward Focused ($75K Phase 2), Strategic ($90K Phase 2), or Enterprise ($110K+ Phase 2) signed within 60 days.** Example: customer → Baseline + Action Brief $24K → Focused within 60 days → pays $51K incremental for Phase 2 ($75K - $24K credit).

### When NOT to offer

- **Never lead with it.** Offer only after a Focused/Strategic/Enterprise pitch gets price pushback.
- Not appropriate before the buyer has seen Baseline findings (stage selection requires grounding).
- Not appropriate for Fortune 500 or board-level-triggered buyers — those close at Focused minimum.

## Phase 2 Anchor: Board-Level Engagement — $275,000 / year

**Role in funnel:** Anchor only. NOT published on the marketing site, in cold email, or in any asset outside sales conversations. Presented **first** in Phase 2 scoping calls with qualified enterprise buyers to reset the price frame before walking the buyer through Strategic and Enterprise. Without this tier, Enterprise at $124K feels like the top of the menu and reads as "the expensive option." With Board-Level at $275K presented first, Enterprise reads as the reasonable middle-market choice.

**It is a real offer Antellion would deliver.** The delivery economics below assume real execution capacity. Do not fake the anchor — present it with genuine enthusiasm as a real option. Hormozi's rule: if you treat the anchor like a fake, the customer will too.

### Who this is for

- $1B+ revenue, 10,000+ headcount (typically 20,000+)
- PE portcos under investor-directed talent transformation, public companies with board-level employer brand reporting, or regulated industries (financial services, healthcare, defense)
- Trigger events: CHRO-led reputation reset, activist investor pressure on talent, post-M&A employer brand integration, public controversy requiring ongoing narrative monitoring, announced intent to hire 3,000+ roles in 12 months
- Already spending $500K-$2M annually on employer brand agencies, careers site platforms, and recruitment marketing

### What's included

- **Quarterly full-scope audits** (5+ categories × 3 layers × 4+ models × all 4 stages) with Journey Action Map refresh and per-category findings reports
- **Unlimited ad-hoc re-scans** during the annual term (10-business-day SLA)
- **Quarterly in-person CHRO/CEO briefing** (Jordan presents; travel included)
- **Annual board-ready deck** with two board or audit-committee readouts per year (Jordan presents)
- **Two competitor war-gaming sessions per year** — 3-hour working sessions with talent + comms + brand leadership
- **Named senior analyst** dedicated for full 12-month term; Jordan as executive sponsor
- **Dedicated Slack Connect channel** with 1-business-day SLA
- **Priority scheduling** ahead of other Antellion customers

### Why $275K

Priced near break-even at delivery cost. Margin role is anchor, not ACV driver. Target: 1-2 sold per year while Phase 2 volume builds. Full delivery-cost breakdown in `docs/offers/board-level-engagement.md`.

### When to present

Always first in Phase 2 scoping with qualified buyers. Sequence: open with Board-Level ($275K), walk through Strategic ($104K) and Enterprise ($124K+) as the "working middle." Walk away from the conversation if the buyer does not match the profile above — leading with the anchor on a mid-market buyer damages trust.

### Contract structure

Single annual SOW under MSA. $68,750 billed quarterly, Net 30. Auto-renewal with 60-day opt-out. MSA and DPA required (no exceptions).

### Referral rev share math (20% of total engagement to referrer)

Because every Phase 2 engagement includes Phase 1 Baseline, and every Phase 2 may also include the $4,900 Diagnostic credit-rollover, referral economics compound:

- Diagnostic-only ($4.9K) → $980 per referral
- Baseline-only ($14K) → $2,800 per referral
- Baseline + Action Brief ($24K) → $4,800 per referral
- Baseline + Focused ($89K total) → **$17,800** per referral
- Baseline + Strategic ($104K total) → **$20,800** per referral
- Baseline + Enterprise ($124K+) → **$24,800+** per referral
- Board-Level Engagement ($275K) → **$55,000** per referral (Year 1)
- Antellion Continuous (post-launch, $78K Y1) → **$15,600** per referral

## Buyer psychology breakpoints (at 5K-employee CPO level)

| Price band | Dynamics | Tier |
|---|---|---|
| Under $5K | P-card or simple PO. CHRO/VP TA authorizes directly, no committee. | Diagnostic ($4,900) |
| $5K-$15K | Discretionary CPO spend. "Vendor tool" risk — mitigated by analyst-delivered framing. | Baseline ($14K) |
| $15K-$50K | CPO approves, loops in finance. Sweet spot for pilot/land engagement. | Baseline + Action Brief ($24K) |
| $75K-$100K | Triggers procurement (4-6 week cycle). Requires board-level narrative. Reads as "strategic initiative." | Focused ($89K), Strategic ($104K) |
| $100K+ | Enterprise procurement, SOC2 questions, committee buying. Budget-cycle dependent. | Enterprise ($124K+) |
| $200K+ annual | Board-level or investor-directed. Annual retainer framing. | Board-Level Engagement ($275K) |

**The jump from $45K to $75K is the painful one.** Worth it — $75K reads "strategic initiative," $45K reads "line item." CPOs protect budgets that sound strategic; they cut line items. The Diagnostic at $4,900 exists specifically to bypass this dynamic entirely — a P-card price point that converts into rollover-credited Baseline demand without ever touching procurement.
