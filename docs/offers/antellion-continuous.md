**Activation fee:** $25,000 (waived with 12-month commitment)
**Delivered by:** Hire Better Talent LLC, d/b/a Antellion
**Status:** **DRAFT — DO NOT LAUNCH.** Signable by June 1, 2026. Launch gated on delivery milestones below.
**Last updated:** 2026-04-23

---

## Purpose

Antellion's first recurring-revenue offer. Converts the post-engagement relationship from ad-hoc re-scans into a standing monthly retainer that funds ongoing narrative monitoring, quarterly deep re-scans, and a priority channel for findings questions.

This offer fills the biggest gap in the current money model — no recurring revenue means every month's customer acquisition cost is funded entirely from project work, which caps growth. Continuous is designed to be presented on the same signature page as Phase 2 so continuity attach happens at the moment of peak trust, not reopened later.

## Why this is a DRAFT

Antellion has not yet delivered a Phase 1 Baseline. Launching a subscription offer before we have:

1. Delivered 2-3 Baselines and observed how customers actually use the deliverable
2. Built automated monitoring alert logic in the product (`apps/web` and `apps/jobs`) — without this, "monthly alerts" is a manual analyst task with bad unit economics
3. Found at least one Phase 2 customer willing to serve as a Continuous reference

...would put Antellion in the position of having sold a subscription that cannot be serviced. That is worse than having no subscription offer.

## What's included

### Quarterly deep re-scan (4 per year)

- Full Snapshot-depth scan across all job categories covered in the customer's most recent Phase 2 engagement
- Narrative drift analysis vs prior quarter
- Citation source change log
- 10-15 page quarterly narrative update report
- 45-minute quarterly findings review call with the analyst

### Monthly monitoring alerts (automated)

Alerts are generated by the Antellion product and delivered by email + Slack. Alert triggers (must be spec'd and built before launch):

- **Sentiment flip** on a candidate-intent query (positive → negative or vice versa) in any tracked model
- **New competitor mention** — competitor appears on a query where they did not previously
- **Citation source drift** — AI cites a new platform for the customer that crosses a reputation-risk threshold (configurable at setup)
- **Hallucination detection** — AI produces factually inconsistent narrative vs confirmed customer facts
- **Narrative inconsistency across models** — two models return materially different characterizations on the same query
- **Zero-presence expansion** — candidate queries where the customer now returns zero results but previously appeared

**Alert delivery:** weekly digest by default; same-day for severity-1 alerts (hallucination, sentiment flip on named-entity query).

**Alert volume expectation:** 5-15 alerts per month for a typical customer at Strategic or Enterprise scope. Tuned during onboarding.

### Quarterly executive briefing (4 per year)

- 60-minute call with the CHRO, VP TA, or designated sponsor
- Walkthrough of quarterly findings and alert summary
- Delivered by the named analyst; Jordan joins 1 per year by default

### Priority question channel

- Dedicated Slack Connect channel or email alias for findings questions between briefings
- **Response SLA: 2 business days** on routine questions
- 5 hours per month of ad-hoc analyst time included (for urgent re-scan of a specific query, competitor intelligence requests, or narrative investigation)

### Access to new query clusters

- As Antellion develops new query clusters (e.g., layoff-narrative queries, DEI-narrative queries, compensation-transparency queries), Continuous customers get first access
- Included in the next scheduled quarterly re-scan at no additional cost

### Product access

- Read-only access to the customer's snapshots and historical scans in `app.antellion.com`
- Tokenized sharing links for internal distribution

## Pricing mechanics

### Standalone pricing (not attached to Phase 2)

- **$6,500 / month** × 12 months = **$78,000 annual commitment**
- **$25,000 activation fee**, waived with 12-month commitment
- **Payment:** monthly invoicing via Stripe, Net 15, ACH or credit card (card payments over $25K subject to 3% surcharge)
- **Total Year 1 if no commitment:** $25,000 + $78,000 = $103,000 (rarely used — commitment path is the default)

### Continuity discount (attached at Phase 2 close)

Customers who sign Continuous on the same signature page as a Phase 2 engagement receive:

- **First quarterly re-scan included at no charge** (~$13,000 standalone value)
- **Effective Year 1 cost: $6,500 × 12 - $13,000 = $65,000**

The continuity discount is the reason to present Continuous at Phase 2 close rather than as a follow-up pitch. Pricing mechanic is lifted from the Hormozi continuity playbook's 2x-ratio dynamic: standalone quarterly re-scan priced at $13,000 (≈ 2 × monthly fee × effective value), which historically drives ~70% attach when presented at the moment of peak trust.

### Waived activation fee

The $25,000 activation fee is real work: onboarding the customer's monitoring baseline, configuring alert thresholds, integrating Slack Connect, setting up quarterly re-scan cadence, and tuning the query library for the customer's industry and candidate personas. Waiving it in exchange for a 12-month commitment is a margin trade Antellion is willing to make because recurring revenue is worth more than activation fee capture at this stage.

Activation work is front-loaded in the first 30 days. If the customer terminates under the termination-for-convenience clause before Year 1 is complete, the activation fee becomes due pro-rata based on unexpired months.

## Cancellation and renewal

### Minimum term

- 12-month initial term from SOW execution
- No cancellation for convenience during the initial 12 months

### After the minimum term

- Converts to **month-to-month**
- Either party may terminate at the end of any month with **60 days' written notice**
- No penalty, no clawback (activation fee is fully earned after month 12)

### Renewal

- Automatic renewal at the then-current list price
- 60-day written notice to prevent renewal at a price increase

### Termination for cause

- Either party may terminate immediately for uncured material breach
- Antellion refunds any prepaid fees for unserviced months

## Total ACV math

Target attach rate: **60% of Phase 2 closes**, calibrated against the 70% Hormozi-table expectation but discounted for early-stage adoption risk.

| Scenario | Monthly new Continuous subscribers | Year 1 ACV | Run-rate ARR by Month 12 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Year 1 conservative (1 Phase 2 close/month × 60% attach) | 0.6 / month | $46,800 added ACV/month | $468K ARR |
| Year 1 target (2 Phase 2 closes/month × 60% attach) | 1.2 / month | $93,600 added ACV/month | $936K ARR |
| Year 2 upside (4 Phase 2 closes/month × 60% attach, higher list price) | 2.4 / month | $187K added ACV/month | $1.87M ARR |

**Unit economics per subscriber:**

- Revenue Year 1: $65K-$78K (depending on attach vs standalone)
- Delivery cost: ~$18K (60 analyst hours annually × $150 + LLM/infra)
- **Contribution margin: ~$47K-$60K / year per subscriber**

**Compounding effect.** After 12 months of steady Phase 2 close rate plus 60% attach, Continuous becomes the dominant margin contributor to the business. This is the long-term compounding play and the reason to ship it in Q2-Q3 even if launch date is conservative.

## Explicit exclusions (must be scoped separately as a Phase 2 engagement or one-off project)

Continuous is a monitoring and light-advisory retainer. It is **not** a replacement for periodic deep engagements. The following require a separate Phase 2 SOW:

- Adding new job categories beyond those in the Phase 2 scope baseline
- Multi-layer conversational deep-dives on a new category
- Strategic deliverables (Journey Action Map refresh beyond annual, new 90-day remediation roadmap, full citation ecosystem re-audit)
- Board or executive presentations (the quarterly briefing is analyst-led; executive presentations are a Board-Level Engagement feature)
- Custom research projects

Making these exclusions explicit protects both the customer (clear scope) and the margin (prevents scope creep into what should be Phase 2 fees).

## SOW structure at launch

Single SOW template, executed under the Antellion MSA. Signable standalone or appended to a Phase 2 SOW.

### Key clauses the SOW must include

1. Scope: 5 monitoring services enumerated above (quarterly re-scans, alerts, briefings, priority channel, query library access)
2. Fee schedule: monthly billing, activation fee waiver, continuity discount
3. SLAs: alert delivery, response time, re-scan delivery, factual accuracy, analyst continuity
4. Term: 12-month minimum, month-to-month thereafter, 60-day notice
5. Termination: for convenience (post-minimum), for cause, effect on activation fee
6. Data handling: DPA required; monitoring alerts may require customer Slack Connect access, which must be scoped in the DPA
7. Price protection: Year 1 fees locked; renewal at then-current list price with 60-day notice
