# Antellion Board-Level Engagement

**Tier:** Phase 2 Anchor — above Enterprise
**Fee:** $275,000 / year (annual engagement, billable quarterly)
**Delivered by:** Hire Better Talent LLC, d/b/a Antellion
**Status:** Internal — not published, used in Phase 2 scoping conversations only
**Last updated:** 2026-04-23

---

## Purpose

This offer is real. Antellion would deliver it if a customer signed for it. Its role in the money model is to reset the price frame before the three-tier Phase 2 menu is presented, so the Enterprise tier ($124K+) reads as the reasonable middle-market choice for a CHRO anchored against a $275K annual engagement.

It is **not** a fake anchor. The deliverables and economics below assume real execution capacity. Present it only in scoping calls with qualified Phase 2 buyers — never on the public site, in cold email, or in any asset that reaches outside the sales conversation.

---

## Who this is for

This engagement fits a narrow profile:

- **Company size.** $1B+ revenue. 10,000+ headcount, typically 20,000+.
- **Ownership.** PE portfolio companies under investor-directed talent transformation, public companies where the CHRO reports to the board on employer brand, or regulated industries (financial services, healthcare, defense) where reputational narrative is tracked at the audit-committee level.
- **Trigger events.** Active CHRO-led reputation reset. Activist investor pressure on talent. Post-M&A employer brand integration. Public controversy requiring ongoing narrative monitoring. Announced intent to hire 3,000+ roles in a 12-month period.
- **Stakeholder.** CHRO who briefs the board on talent; CEO or CMO directly sponsoring employer brand transformation; Chief Communications Officer in regulated industries.
- **Budget reality.** Already spending $500K-$2M annually on employer brand agencies, careers site platforms, and recruitment marketing. Antellion is additive strategic measurement, not replacement of those programs.

If the buyer does not match this profile, do not present this offer. Lead with Enterprise.

## What the engagement includes

### Core measurement (quarterly, four times per year)

- **Quarterly full-scope AI visibility audit** across 5+ job categories, 3 layers of conversational depth, 4 AI models (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, plus any others the customer specifies), all four candidate journey stages
- **Quarterly Journey Action Map refresh** tracking narrative drift and remediation progress
- **Per-category findings reports** (40+ pages each)
- **Rollup synthesis report** integrating findings across categories
- **Unlimited ad-hoc re-scans** during the annual term (for news cycles, leadership changes, crisis response) — scoped per request with a 10-business-day turnaround

### Executive engagement

- **Quarterly in-person CHRO/CEO briefing.** Jordan presents findings at the customer's offices (or remote if preferred). Travel included in fee.
- **Annual board-ready deck.** Full-cycle narrative of AI visibility position, competitive trajectory, and remediation outcomes. Editable source files delivered.
- **Two board or audit-committee readouts** per year. Jordan presents.
- **Competitor war-gaming sessions.** Two per year. 3-hour working sessions with customer's talent, communications, and brand leadership team to model how competitor narrative shifts affect customer's candidate funnel. Includes custom scenario analysis.

### Named account continuity

- **Named senior analyst** assigned to the account for the full annual term. No rotating analysts, no account-team churn.
- **Jordan as executive sponsor** for the full term. Single point of accountability.

### Priority access

- **Dedicated Slack Connect channel** with the named analyst and Jordan. 1-business-day response SLA on findings questions.
- **Direct email access** to the analyst and Jordan.
- **Priority scheduling** for ad-hoc re-scans, briefings, or war-gaming sessions ahead of other Antellion customers.

### Optional additions (scoped separately)

- Custom research beyond standard methodology (diversity narrative deep-dive, M&A employer brand analysis, regulatory compliance narratives)
- International scope (non-English AI models, country-specific candidate personas)
- Additional board or shareholder-facing presentations beyond the two included

## Why $275,000

The fee reflects the actual delivery cost at this depth, not a round number chosen for anchoring. Internal math:

| Delivery component | Hours / cost |
|---|---|
| Quarterly audits (5 categories × 3 layers × 4 models × 4 quarters, including analyst synthesis) | ~240 analyst hours × $150/hr loaded = $36,000 |
| Unlimited ad-hoc re-scans (budget 6-8 per year at ~15 hrs each) | ~105 hours × $150 = $15,750 |
| Quarterly CHRO/CEO briefings (4 × full-day prep + delivery + travel) | ~80 hours × $200 blended executive rate = $16,000 + $12,000 travel = $28,000 |
| Board readouts (2 × high-prep events) | ~50 hours × $250 = $12,500 |
| Competitor war-gaming sessions (2 × full-day scenario prep + facilitation) | ~60 hours × $250 = $15,000 |
| Named analyst continuity premium (retention and dedication cost) | ~$25,000 annually |
| Priority Slack Connect + email SLA (budget 4 hrs/week × 48 weeks × $150) | ~$28,800 |
| Annual board deck (full-cycle narrative, editable artifacts) | ~60 hours × $200 = $12,000 |
| LLM API costs (full-scope audits plus ad-hoc scans, ~4,000 queries × 4 models × 3 layers × 5 categories) | ~$8,000-12,000 |
| Software, infrastructure, security review cooperation, DPA execution, SOC2 readiness | ~$15,000 |
| Executive sponsor time (Jordan, ~5 hrs/week × 48 weeks × $400) | ~$96,000 |
| **Total delivery cost** | **~$271,000-275,000** |

**Contribution margin at list price: near break-even at the delivery-cost line.** This is by design. The offer is priced to be real and deliverable, not to be a profit center at this stage. Its role in the money model is the anchor, not the ACV driver. Deliver 1-2 per year while Phase 2 volume builds.

## When to present

**Always present first in any Phase 2 scoping conversation with a customer who fits the profile above.**

Sequence:

1. Phase 1 Baseline delivered. Findings readout complete.
2. Phase 2 scoping call opens.
3. Jordan opens with: "Given your scale and the board-level narrative pressure you've described, I want to walk you through three levels at which we can engage in Phase 2. The top-end is what we call a Board-Level Engagement — annual retainer, quarterly presentations at your offices, named senior analyst, unlimited re-scans. It runs $275,000 a year. Most customers at your scale land between our Strategic and Enterprise tiers, which I'll walk through after. But I want to show you the top first so you can see the full architecture."
4. Walk through Board-Level Engagement for 5-7 minutes with genuine enthusiasm. Treat it as a real option, not a prop.
5. Then present Strategic ($104K) and Enterprise ($124K+) as the working middle.

**Why this sequence works.** Enterprise buyers respond to choice architecture, not single-option pressure. Presenting the full range — including a top end that's deliberately larger than the buyer's budget — signals Antellion operates at real enterprise scale and lets the buyer self-locate without being pushed. A CHRO who came into the call thinking Enterprise at $124K was the upper limit now reads Enterprise as the reasonable choice within a larger menu.

## What Antellion commits to

If a customer signs the Board-Level Engagement, the following commitments are contractually binding in the SOW:

- **Analyst continuity.** The named senior analyst is dedicated for the full 12-month term. If the analyst leaves Antellion during the term, Jordan personally backfills the role until a replacement is onboarded and the customer has approved the replacement in writing.
- **Response SLA.** 1 business day on Slack and email. Measured quarterly. Failure to meet the SLA in more than 10% of measurable interactions in a quarter triggers a 5% quarterly-fee credit.
- **Delivery windows.** Quarterly deliverables ship within stated schedules. Delivery slips of more than 5 business days trigger a 10% credit on the affected quarter.
- **Ad-hoc re-scan SLA.** 10 business days from request, assuming customer provides scope within 2 business days.
- **Confidentiality and data handling.** Standard Antellion DPA plus any customer-specific security addenda. SOC2 readiness cooperation included.
- **Factual accuracy.** Same as all tiers: any factual error corrected within 48 hours or refunded.

## What is explicitly NOT included (must be scoped separately)

- Creative production (writing blog posts, creating videos, building landing pages) — Antellion measures and recommends, it does not execute brand content
- Technical implementation on customer systems (ATS changes, careers site edits, SEO changes)
- Media relations or crisis PR
- Employee surveys, engagement studies, or internal culture work
- ATS or HRIS integrations

These belong with the customer's existing employer brand agencies, recruitment marketing partners, careers site platform vendors, and internal comms teams. Antellion's role is to tell those teams — with evidence — where AI's narrative about the company is strongest, weakest, and most at risk, so the programs they already run are directed with more precision.

## Contract structure

- **Single annual SOW** under Antellion MSA
- **Billing:** $68,750 quarterly, invoiced on the first business day of each quarter, Net 30
- **Term:** 12 months from SOW execution
- **Renewal:** Automatic annual renewal at list price (then-current) unless either party gives 60-day written notice of non-renewal
- **Termination for convenience:** Either party may terminate at the end of any quarter with 60 days' notice. Customer pays for the quarter in which notice is given plus any deliverables in flight.
- **Data handling:** Antellion DPA plus customer-specific security addenda. SOC2 Type II cooperation timeline stated in the SOW if requested.
