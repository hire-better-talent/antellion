**Decision (2026-04-16):** Focused Audit standard scope shifts from 4 layers to 2 layers (Discovery + Consideration). Price drops from $18K to $15K. Evaluation and Commitment layers become $6K Deep Dive add-ons.

**Why:** Jordan challenged the 4-layer default as vendor-led complexity. On honest review: Discovery + Consideration produce ~80% of the remediation actions a buyer will actually take. Evaluation is insight-rich but action-light (fixes overlap Consideration). Commitment is nice-to-have for 90% of buyers — interesting for offer-decline diagnostics but rarely triggers a 90-day plan. Pricing all 4 layers into every deal risks overserving, churn signal, and overpromising.

**How to apply:**
- Standard Focused Audit = 1 category × 2 layers × 3 models at $15K, 10-12 hr delivery, 2-week turnaround
- Evaluation Deep Dive and Commitment Deep Dive = $6K each as expansion/add-on
- Positions a cleaner annual revenue story: Focused Audit → Deep Dive add-on → re-scan
- The 4-stage candidate journey framework stays intact in blog/narrative — just not every buyer needs all 4 in their first engagement
- Supersedes prior $18K × 4-layer framing in project_pricing_tiers.md and project_assessment_packaging_v1.md (update those if they go out to buyers)
