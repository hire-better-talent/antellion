Jordan shifted outreach strategy twice on 2026-04-09:
1. From manual per-prospect research to a self-serve model where prospects visit antellion.com and request their own Visibility Snapshot
2. From LinkedIn DMs to cold email as the primary outreach channel

**Why:** The manual approach does not scale for a solo founder. LinkedIn outreach is off-limits because Jordan's profile shows Symphony Talent (cannot be changed due to employment risk), which creates cognitive dissonance when pitching Antellion.

**How to apply:**
- All outreach is cold email from jordan@antellion.com or hello@antellion.com
- CTA in all emails directs to antellion.com for self-serve snapshot request
- No LinkedIn DMs for Antellion outreach
- Email copy should establish Jordan as an independent researcher/founder without needing LinkedIn corroboration
- Prospect list building via Apollo.io or similar (VP TA, Head of Employer Brand, CPO at 1K-10K employee companies)
- Target: 80-100 cold emails to generate 3-8 snapshot requests for model validation
