Email warmup and cold email launch plan created 2026-04-12.

Key decisions:
- Google Workspace for email provider (deliverability trust, Apollo integration)
- Warmbox for inbox warmup (native Google Workspace support, dedicated warmup network)
- 14-day minimum warmup period before any cold sends
- Warmup runs permanently in parallel with cold sends (never turned off)
- Cold send ramp: 5/day week 1, 8-10/day week 2, 15-20/day week 3+
- Hard ceiling of 30-35 total emails/day from single mailbox
- Target launch date: April 29, 2026 (Tuesday)

Parallel work during warmup:
1. Build Apollo list (days 1-3)
2. Run industry research scans for 3-4 verticals (days 3-7)
3. Write and publish 2-3 foundation content pieces from GEO plan (days 4-10)
4. Landing page optimization (days 5-7)
5. Response infrastructure prep (days 8-10)

**Why:** Brand new domain with zero sending history cannot send cold email without destroying deliverability permanently. The 14-day warmup is non-negotiable per both the campaign plan and cold email best practices.

**How to apply:** All future cold email volume decisions, second mailbox timing, and deliverability troubleshooting should reference this plan. The ramp schedule and monitoring thresholds are the baseline.
