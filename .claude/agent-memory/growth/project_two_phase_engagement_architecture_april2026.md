## Decision (2026-04-19)

Every paid Antellion engagement is structured in two phases:

- **Phase 1: Baseline Audit** ($14,000) — mandatory first step. 1 category × 1 layer × 3 models × all 4 candidate journey stages. Human-interpreted report + executive readout. Delivered in 2 weeks.
- **Phase 2 (optional, scoped against Baseline findings):**
  - Focused: +$75K (total engagement $89K)
  - Strategic: +$90K (total engagement $104K)
  - Enterprise: +$110K+ (total engagement starts at $124K)

**The customer may elect to stop at Baseline.** Baseline stands alone as a complete deliverable. Phase 2 is never sold as a commitment — it is priced after Phase 1 is delivered and the customer decides whether to proceed based on what Baseline revealed.

## Why

This is the McKinsey / BCG / Deloitte engagement architecture: establish the fact pattern before committing to execution. Reasons:

1. **Risk reduction for the buyer.** Procurement signs $14K more easily than $89K. Phase 1 delivers proof. Phase 2 is pre-sold.
2. **Better Phase 2 scope.** Phase 1 reveals what the customer actually cares about. Phase 2 is priced against real findings, not guesses.
3. **Delivery consistency.** Every engagement starts the same way. Antellion gets operationally good at Phase 1 fast; Phase 2 is customized but starts from a known foundation.
4. **Methodology demonstration.** The customer sees Antellion's actual work product before committing $75K+.
5. **Compounding revenue.** Every Focused deal becomes $89K ($14K + $75K), not $75K. Average deal size goes up, not down.
6. **Natural upsell.** Phase 1 findings create the specific case for Phase 2. "You saw this at 1 layer. Here's what 3 layers would reveal."

## Rename

"Starter Audit" is **not the correct term** — replaced with "Baseline Audit" throughout. Rationale: Starter implies small/entry-level; Baseline implies foundational and measurable. Baseline also supports the tracking narrative ("re-scan against Baseline in 90 days").

## How to apply

- **In pitch decks / proposals:** lead with "Every engagement begins with a Baseline Audit." Frame Phase 2 options as "priced against Baseline findings."
- **In response emails (Template #9):** already updated. Anchors $14K Baseline first, then tier deltas.
- **In referral conversations:** rev share is 20% of **total engagement** (Baseline + Phase 2). A Sarika-referred client landing at Baseline + Focused = $89K × 20% = $17.8K.
- **In procurement conversations:** package as single SOW with two phases. Single procurement cycle, sequential delivery.
- **In risk reversal language:** Baseline guarantee now includes "customer not obligated to proceed to Phase 2." Strengthens the no-pressure framing.
