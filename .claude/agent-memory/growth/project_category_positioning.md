Category name: "AI Employer Visibility" -- not "employer brand intelligence," "talent SEO," or "AI talent analytics."

Category-defining statement: "Antellion is an AI employer visibility platform. We show you how ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity describe your company when candidates ask where to work -- and what to do when the answer isn't good enough."

Key differentiators from adjacent categories:
- vs. employer branding agencies: "We show what AI already tells candidates, whether you wrote it or not"
- vs. Glassdoor/Comparably: "We show what AI says, which synthesizes hundreds of sources into one answer"
- vs. SEO tools: "AI doesn't rank pages -- it synthesizes answers. Different surface."
- vs. HR tech: "We manage how you show up before the candidate ever reaches your workflow"

**Why:** The positioning must create a new mental category so buyers compare Antellion to nothing, not to existing tools.

**How to apply:** All copy, outreach, sales decks, and landing pages should use "AI employer visibility" as the category. Avoid falling back into "talent optimization," "employer brand analytics," or other commodity language.
