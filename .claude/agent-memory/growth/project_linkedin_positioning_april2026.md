Critical constraint (2026-04-09): Jordan cannot publicly associate himself with Antellion on LinkedIn. His LinkedIn profile must continue to show Symphony Talent only. No headline change, no Antellion in experience section, no Antellion-branded posts from personal account.

Previous strategy (same date, earlier session) recommended "Founder, Antellion | AI Employer Visibility" as LinkedIn headline. That strategy is now explicitly superseded.

**Why:** Listing Antellion on LinkedIn creates employment risk at Symphony Talent. The ICP overlap is near-total -- Symphony Talent and Antellion sell to the same VP TA / employer brand buyers. Any public association could trigger conflict-of-interest concerns.

**How to apply:**
- Primary outreach channel is cold email from jordan@antellion.com (completely separated from LinkedIn)
- LinkedIn DMs are off-limits for Antellion outreach (profile shows Symphony Talent, creates cognitive dissonance)
- An Antellion company LinkedIn page is recommended as a credibility backstop, but Jordan should NOT be listed as an employee on it
- If a prospect asks about the Symphony Talent connection, the response framework is: "Antellion is an independent research project. My day job is in the employer brand space, which is how I spotted the AI visibility gap." Honest framing that adds credibility.
- All outreach copy, landing page, and materials must stand on their own without requiring the prospect to check LinkedIn
- Occasional personal LinkedIn posts about AI/hiring trends (no Antellion mention, no link) are acceptable as light awareness building but NOT as a lead gen channel
