Open Forge AI (openforge.ai) is the first confirmed direct-ish competitor in the AI visibility space. Key facts as of 2026-04-15:

- **Product:** Platform for measuring and improving brand visibility in AI answer engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity). Combines visibility tracking, competitor monitoring, citation analytics, content generation, and "Full AEO Automation."
- **Pricing:** $499/month, unlimited usage and users. 20% off annual.
- **ICP:** Marketing teams, demand gen leaders, agencies, multi-location brands. NOT targeting employer brand, TA, or HR buyers.
- **Case study:** Quilter (electronics design company), Director of Marketing. Claims +467.8% ChatGPT traffic, +300% "Forge Score" (their proprietary visibility metric), ~7 hours/week saved, 1-to-5x content output increase.
- **Proprietary metric:** "Forge Score" -- single number for brand visibility in ChatGPT. Black box, no methodology disclosed.
- **Contact:** Jason, jason@openforge.ai
- **Report for Symphony Talent:** Open Forge produced a 10-page AI Visibility Report for Symphony Talent analyzing 288 queries, 23 brand mentions, 12 citations, 4.2% citation rate. Recommendations were generic content marketing tactics (get reviews, Wikipedia, "best tools" lists, Reddit presence). Report quality was mediocre -- fabricated uplift projections, muddled competitive framing (compared ST to ATS platforms rather than recruitment marketing peers).

**Why this matters:**
1. Confirms the AI visibility category is real and getting crowded on the B2B marketing side
2. The employer brand / candidate-intent vertical remains unoccupied by Open Forge or visible competitors
3. Symphony Talent (Jordan's employer) is now a recipient of an AI visibility report, meaning the concept is landing near Jordan's day job
4. Open Forge's automated report-as-prospecting-asset model is validated and worth considering for Antellion

**How to apply:**
- Antellion's differentiation is vertical (employer/candidate) not horizontal (generic brand visibility)
- Need a branded visibility metric equivalent to "Forge Score"
- Consider a lightweight "AI Employer Visibility Snapshot" as a cold prospecting asset
- Antellion's recommendations must be more specific than Open Forge's generic content marketing playbook -- tie to employer brand strategy, EVP, careers site, Glassdoor narrative
- Watch for signs that Open Forge or similar players pivot into the employer brand vertical
- Monitor whether Symphony Talent leadership acts on the Open Forge report or builds an internal AI visibility capability
